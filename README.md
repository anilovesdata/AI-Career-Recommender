# AI Career Recommender for Nigerian University Students

A simple, friendly AI chatbot that helps university students in Nigeria choose or change the right course and career path.

## The Problem in Nigeria
Many university students pick courses because of family pressure, peer influence, or limited information.  
This often leads to:
- Wrong course choices
- High dropout rates
- Wasted time and money
- Graduates who struggle to find relevant jobs in a difficult labour market

## How the AI Helps
Students describe their interests and strengths in plain language, for example:
- “I like biology, helping people, and using computers”
- “I’m strong in mathematics, physics and I enjoy building things”

The AI then recommends suitable **university courses** and **career paths** with clear reasons. It combines:
- The student’s interests, hobbies, academic strengths and personality traits
- Real Nigerian job market demand (tech, AI, healthcare, engineering, data science, banking, etc.)

## Why This Project Matters
It gives practical, realistic guidance so students can make better decisions early and graduate with better chances of employment.

## Technologies Planned
- Python (for the core logic)
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) – to understand what students write
- Later: Streamlit or Gradio to create a simple chatbot interface
